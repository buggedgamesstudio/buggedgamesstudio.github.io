About the game

TOASTER is a fun and addictive simulator where you take on the role of a toaster and manage the entire bread toasting process.

Toast different types of bread, each with unique properties and behavior. Some breads are soft and quick, others are thick and require careful timing to get the perfect golden crunch.

Discover special breads with bonus effects — Golden bread multiplies your coins, Gray bread is extra challenging, and the Devilish bread will keep you on your toes. Each variety has its own personality.

Earn coins for every perfectly toasted slice and use them to unlock new bread types for your collection. Track your progress and fill out the Breadpedia — an in-game encyclopedia of all the breads you've discovered.

TOASTER is being developed as a lighthearted and satisfying game that lets you enjoy the simple pleasure of a perfectly toasted slice, experiment with rare breads, and build your ultimate collection.

Toast. Collect. Discover new breads. Build your perfect Breadpedia.
