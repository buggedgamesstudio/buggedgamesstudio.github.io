About the game

You are the cursor. Not a tool. Not a command. An error.

The world around you is a system that runs on strict rules. But your movement obeys none of them. The system noticed.

The spheres that appear in waves are not enemies in the usual sense. They are cleanup processes. They analyze, chase, and attempt to synchronize you with the system.

The longer you exist, the more aggressively the system adapts. It does not get angry. It corrects.

You cannot attack directly. You can only survive, maneuver, and use the environment to destroy what hunts you.
